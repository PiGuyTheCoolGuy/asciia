# Asciia: Mission Package System

Asciia is a virtual RPG-style mission system where players (called _rovers_) complete structured missions guided by _ground control_ (the real-world players) and a _relay space station_ (the DM or game master role).

The game focuses on coordination, strategy, and progression through objective-based missions rather than traditional combat-heavy RPG systems.

---

## Core Concept

A **Mission Package** is the equivalent of a campaign in Asciia.

Each mission package contains:

- A map (world or environment layout)
- Rovers (player-controlled units/characters)
- NPCs (non-player characters)
- Items and resources
- Objectives and mission goals

Players must work together to guide rovers through a series of objectives, gather resources, and ultimately complete the mission.

---

## Mission Structure

Each mission is divided into **Objectives**:

- Objectives are small, actionable tasks
- Completing objectives contributes to overall mission progress
- Some objectives unlock or simplify others when completed early
- Resource collection is key to efficiency and success

### Example Objective Types

- Exploration (discover locations)
- Resource gathering (collect materials)
- Repair tasks (restore systems or structures)
- Data recovery (retrieve information)
- Escort or transport missions

---

## Gameplay Loop

1. Mission begins at a **drop point**
2. Ground control assigns commands to rovers
3. Rovers travel the map and complete objectives
4. Resources and items are collected along the way
5. Objectives are completed in strategic order
6. Final objective: return to drop point and complete mission extraction

---

## Strategy & Progression

- Early resource gathering is critical for success
- Some objectives become easier as resources are acquired
- Efficient planning improves mission success rate
- Coordination between ground control and relay station is essential

---

## Roles

### Ground Control

The players managing rover actions:

- Issue commands
- Plan routes
- Prioritize objectives
- Manage resources

### Relay Space Station (DM Role)

The facilitator of the mission:

- Controls NPC behavior
- Provides mission updates
- Manages map events
- Enforces mission rules and progression
